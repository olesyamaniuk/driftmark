Color Field

Pull the Right Color. Fill the Grid.

You'll drag colorful ropes through puzzles that keep changing - walls move, colors shift, and finding the perfect path gets tricky fast.

Features

  • The rope stuff is actually pretty tricky

    This isn't your typical drag-and-drop game. You've got to weave ropes through obstacles without tangling them up or missing any spots.

  • New obstacles pop up to mess with you

    Moving Walls shift the board mid-solve, and Color Changers force you to rethink paths you thought were locked in.

  • Tons of really hard puzzles

    The game starts easy to teach you the basics, then throws curveballs that'll make you rethink everything.

  • Precision Grid Design

    We made sure every puzzle actually has a real solution - no random guessing required.

How It Works

1.
Survey the Grid
Each level shows colored endpoints scattered across a tiled board — your job is to connect matching pairs.
2.
Drag Your Ropes
Draw paths from one colored tile to its match, filling every cell in the grid without letting paths cross.
3.
Beat the Obstacles
Dodge Moving Walls, use Color Changers strategically, and clear the board to complete the puzzle.

FAQ

  • Is Color Field free to play?
    Yes, Color Field is free to download and play on Android. Some optional features or level packs may be available within the app.
  • Do I need an internet connection to play?
    You can play most levels without wifi, so it's perfect for the subway, doctor's office, or just lounging around at home.
  • How hard do the puzzles get?
    There are hundreds of puzzles starts super easy but gets crazy hard later — expect the difficulty to build steadily.
  • What makes Color Changers and Moving Walls different from standard obstacles?
    Color Changers mess with your rope's color halfway through, so you'll need to completely rethink your approach. Moving Walls shift the available grid space, so timing and planning matter as much as logic.
